Windowsills

Exterior windowsills:

Class EXCLUSIVE, Extruded aluminium 2mm

The use:

Extruded aluminum sills represent the ideal solution for exterior window finalisation. In addition to the aesthetic look of the house, they overlap the outer veneer lining, which is designed to drain down the running water and prevent the facade of the house from getting dirty. The exterior aluminum sills supplied by us are fulfilling the highest requirements for exterior windowsills. They are suitable for the toughest weather conditions.

Colour variations:

white RAL 9016
brown RAL 8019
elox bronze C33
natural elox C0
other RAL

Maintenance:

After installation of the sill, remove the protective foil. Window sills can be cleaned with household cleaners. We do not recommend to use chemical cleaners.

Advantages:

  • High strength
  • Resistance to harsh weather
  • Low thermal expansion
  • Resistance to corrosion
  • Long-life, maintenace free and without the need for repaint, High mechanical resistance
  • Color stability
  • Unlimited range of RAL color variations
